## The Invention of Race

Race, as understood in the modern Western world, is not a biological reality — it is a social and political construct with a traceable history. The concept of distinct human "races" arranged in a hierarchy was invented in the 17th and 18th centuries, coinciding precisely with the expansion of the transatlantic slave trade. This was not a coincidence.

## The Architects of Racial Science

### Carl Linnaeus (1707–1778)
The Swedish botanist who created the modern system of biological classification also divided humans into four racial categories, assigning behavioral and intellectual traits to each. Europeans were described as "gentle, acute, inventive" while Africans were labeled "crafty, indolent, negligent." These characterizations had no empirical basis — they were simply the prejudices of an 18th-century European encoded in scientific language.

### Samuel Morton (1799–1851)
Morton collected over 1,000 human skulls and claimed to demonstrate that skull size — and therefore brain size and intelligence — varied by race, with Europeans having the largest skulls and Africans the smallest. His work was enormously influential. It was also wrong. Stephen Jay Gould's reanalysis in *The Mismeasure of Man* (1981) revealed that Morton's measurements were systematically biased. Even when later researchers re-measured Morton's collection with modern tools, the racial differences he claimed were either nonexistent or fell within normal variation.

### The Eugenics Movement
In the early 20th century, the eugenics movement — which sought to improve the human gene pool through selective breeding — was mainstream science, endorsed by universities, funded by major foundations, and implemented in law. Eugenics directly led to forced sterilization programs that targeted Black, Indigenous, and disabled Americans. Between 1907 and 1963, over 64,000 Americans were forcibly sterilized. North Carolina's eugenics program, which ran until 1977, disproportionately targeted Black women.

The same pseudoscience was later adopted by Nazi Germany as the intellectual justification for the Holocaust.

## The Bell Curve and Modern Pseudoscience

In 1994, Richard Herrnstein and Charles Murray published *The Bell Curve*, arguing that IQ differences between racial groups were partially genetic. The book was widely promoted despite devastating criticism from geneticists, psychologists, and statisticians who identified fundamental methodological flaws:

- IQ tests measure a narrow range of cognitive abilities shaped heavily by education, nutrition, and environment
- The "heritability" of IQ within a group tells you nothing about differences between groups
- The authors conflated correlation with causation throughout
- The racial categories used have no consistent genetic basis

Yet *The Bell Curve* continues to be cited by those seeking scientific cover for racist beliefs, demonstrating how persistent pseudoscience can be once it enters public discourse.

## What Genetics Actually Shows

Modern genomics has definitively answered the question that racial science purported to address. The Human Genome Project and subsequent research have established that:

- All humans share 99.9% of their DNA
- There is more genetic variation within so-called racial groups than between them
- The genes associated with skin color represent a tiny fraction of the genome and have no correlation with intelligence, behavior, or moral character
- Human populations exist on a continuum — there are no clear genetic boundaries that correspond to racial categories

The concept of biological race has been rejected by the American Association of Physical Anthropologists, the American Society of Human Genetics, and virtually every major scientific organization that has examined the evidence.

## The Persistence of Myth

Despite the scientific consensus, racial pseudoscience persists — in online forums, in policy discussions, and in the assumptions that undergird systems from criminal justice to education. Understanding the history of how these myths were manufactured is essential to dismantling them. They were never real science. They were always political tools dressed in the language of objectivity.
